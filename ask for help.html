WHY IS IT IMPORTANT TO ASK FOR HELP?



The more you ask for help, the easier it becomes and the better you get at it. Here are some things you can do to ask for help:



  • Ask your teachers. -- You can ask your adviser as they know more about the topic that you need to know.



  • Ask your classmates. -- You can ask for help from your classmates who have advanced studies in the things you want to know.



  • Be as clear as possible about what you need. -- Try to make your request simple and specific. If you're not sure exactly what kind of help you need.



  • Ask experts. -- You can ask help to an expert since they are more knowlegdeable and can give you more ideas.



  • Ask your family. -- You can ask your family member to give you some help if you are really having a hard time to do something.
